This week, Amos and Swan dropped theirs.

That's launch energy in 2026.

And Denver knows that feeling. Because behind every new restaurant, SaaS tool, fitness studio, creative agency, and weird beautiful side project in this city… there's someone betting on a model that shouldn't work. Someone choosing courage over comfort. Systems over excuses. Signal over noise.

Amos is proving you can scale with intelligence, not payroll. And all across Denver, founders are doing their own version of that math. Smaller teams. Sharper tools. Bigger swings.

This week in Real Good Denver, we're spotlighting the launches. The bold ones. The quiet ones. The "we're just going to try it" ones.

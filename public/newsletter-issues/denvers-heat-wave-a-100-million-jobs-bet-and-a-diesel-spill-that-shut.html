Weekly Denver Forecast

Denver is locked into a genuine heat dome this week, with highs holding in the upper 90s (99 on Tuesday, dipping to a slightly more humane 90 by Monday) and overnight lows stuck in the mid to upper 60s, so there's zero relief after dark either. Wildfire smoke from the state's ongoing fires has already pushed Denver's air quality past Los Angeles's once this month, so keep the windows shut and save the guilt for the water bill instead of the sprinklers.

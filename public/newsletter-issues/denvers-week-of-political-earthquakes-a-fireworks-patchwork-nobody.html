Denver Volunteer Opportunity

We Don't Waste, Mobile Food Market Volunteer - LINK 
With Denver in the middle of a Stage 1 drought and food insecurity numbers climbing statewide, We Don't Waste needs hands to help distribute rescued surplus food at its Mobile Food Markets around the metro area.

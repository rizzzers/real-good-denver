Weekly Denver Forecast

Denver is spending the week flirting with the century mark, with Monday's 100 degree high giving way to a run of afternoon thunderstorm chances in the mid to upper 80s and low 90s through Thursday. Expect a stray storm Friday before the ridge builds back in for a sunny, hot weekend with highs climbing back into the mid to upper 90s by Sunday.
